# 📊 Customer Segmentation using RFM Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project applies **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis** to segment customers based on their purchasing behavior. The goal is to identify high-value customers, understand spending patterns, and support data-driven marketing decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this analysis is to:

* Identify high-value and loyal customers
* Improve customer retention strategies
* Enable targeted marketing campaigns
* Increase overall business profitability

---

## 🧠 What is RFM Analysis?

RFM stands for:

* **Recency (R):** How recently a customer made a purchase
* **Frequency (F):** How often a customer makes purchases
* **Monetary (M):** How much money a customer spends

These metrics help in understanding customer behavior and value.

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

1. Data Cleaning and Preparation
2. Calculation of Recency, Frequency, and Monetary values
3. Scoring customers based on quantiles
4. Creating an overall RFM Score
5. Segmenting customers into:

   * High Value
   * Mid Value
   * Low Value

---

## 📈 Visualizations

The analysis includes:

* Bar Chart of Customer Segments
* Histogram of Monetary Distribution
* Boxplot for Outlier Detection

These visualizations help in identifying spending patterns and customer distribution.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Customer spending is unevenly distributed, with a small group contributing significantly to revenue
* Majority of customers fall into the mid-value segment, indicating growth potential
* Presence of high-value outliers highlights the importance of customer retention strategies

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* **High Value Customers:**
  Focus on retention through loyalty programs and exclusive offers

* **Mid Value Customers:**
  Target with upselling and personalized marketing

* **Low Value Customers:**
  Re-engage using discounts and promotional campaigns

---

## ⚠️ Limitations

* RFM analysis does not consider customer satisfaction
* External factors like seasonality are not included
* Behavioral context is limited to transaction data only

---

## ✅ Conclusion

RFM analysis provides a structured approach to customer segmentation and helps businesses make informed decisions. By targeting each segment effectively, companies can improve retention, optimize marketing strategies, and maximize revenue.
